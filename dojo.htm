Althouse Karate and Kickboxing Academy started their kickboxing program 14 years ago. With Master Irv’s expert instruction the program has grown and so has its list of accomplishments. It started with Master Irv’s wish to train full contact fighters. With a little help from Grand Master Kates and a few willing fighters, Master Irv’s kickboxers were fighting on a regular basis. The next accomplishment was becoming a certified promoter. Shortly after that we moved into our 6000 square foot dojo and started holding our own fights. Since then Master Irv has become the representative for the international kickboxing team and has taken fighters to Greece and Africa to compete with great success!

     This program is also a great way to get in shape. Although it is full contact you are not required to fight in the ring or even have a martial arts background. So if this is something that sounds exciting to you let one of our instructors know and we will get you started ASAP.

Master Irv Althouse opened his own school in the beginning of 1995. Over the past 30 years he has trained many students and produced some very good black belt instructors. Master Irv has always believed in a family oriented dojo. Some of our first students are now teaching the martial arts to their own children and even grand children. Our karate program is for students ages 6 and up. Once again if this is something that you would like to do call us now!




     The program focuses  on teaching basic martial arts skills in a fun but structured learning environment. Although rank advancement is included in the program, it takes a back seat to learning skills and having fun!
