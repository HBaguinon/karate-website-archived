MERRY CHRISTMAS and HAPPY HOLIDAYS from
Althouse Karate & Kickboxing Academy!

Here at Althouse Karate & Kickboxing Academy, we provide the highest
level of karate and full-contact kickboxing instruction. Our students are
both young and old coming from all walks of life and experiences.
At AKKA our school does not require any contracts to keep you locked in.
We encourage you and your family to come in and talk with one of our
instructors, take a tour of our school, and meet with some of our students
to decide which program best fits your needs. We hope to show you
how the martial arts can enhance and change your life.
